The Difference Between BungeeBones & Pay Per Click?
  • BungeeBones is a "Pay Per Action" model
    We believe Pay Per Action is fairer to webmasters. As a webmaster there are only two types of visitors to your website - those with their own website and those that without one. Since BungeeBones' product is web traffic both of us interested in getting the former group to join BungeeBones. We do offer various levels of membership (including free) but when someone joins as a paying member then "the action" triggers a payment to the webmaster.
  • BungeeBones Pays Through Multiple Levels
    If someone who joins BungeeBones from your site adds a distributed web directory to their site BungeeBones pays you overide commissions on their ad sales too.
  • BungeeBones Pays Residual Income
    You commissions EACH time the advertiser advertises, not just one time.
  • More respectful Of Your Customers/Visitors
    BungeeBones does not place its links (or ads) on the same page as your content or products. Rather, the Distributed Directory is installed on its own page leaving your site visitor to review your site uninterupted and without other distractive ads to competitors.

Instructions For Adding Your Link
  • Steps To Add Your Link To BungeeBones
    1. Use the combination of our directory and the Regional Filters dropdown menus below to migrate to the category you want to add your link to. Notice the one or two numbers beside each category. They represent the link population (i.e. your competition) in the category and the combined total in its subcategories respectively. Those totals will be updated for you if and/or when a regional filter is selected, reporting how many links there are in that category and THAT region.
    2. If you feel adding regional filters to your link listing is a benefit then use the four dropdowns to select the LOWEST level region you would settle for your link to be displayed in. Note your link will be displayed at higher level regions if populations limits allow (i.e. a link in Los Angeles will be displayed in Californua requests if there are fewer than twenty links in that category in California). When you have arrived at the best location for your link, use the drop down menues to select the best regional filters that apply to your website and/or business (if applicable). Notice the links that appear. Clicking a link will refresh the page and will display only the links from that region and catgegory. NOT SELECTING a region places your link in the "global" (World Wide Web) level.
    3. If your category or region is not listed then proceed as normal and add your link to the "next-best" category and/or region anyway. Then, if you don't mind, drop us a quick note using our contact form along with your link url and your requested category or region and we will do everything we can to accomodate your request. If the suggestion is approved we are glad to move your link into the new category/region for you.
    4. If you are satisfied that you have arrived where you want to place your link then scroll down to the very bottom of the page to add your link and other information to the form, perhaps noticing the other links in the category as you scroll. This is an excellent time to volunteer and help BungeeBones achieve the top quality it is aiming for by visiting the sites and rating them according to their relevancy to the category. Not only does "Peer Rating" help improve the directory's quality but you also get to see other websites and what features they have.
    5. Lastly, the script will present a confirmation page before final submittal. At that time it may also provide additional information, if applicable, such as the number of paid/free links in the category and/or the regions.
 
After the confirmation page the link is submitted and the success page delivers the code to the distributed directory that you can then copy and paste into your web page template.
